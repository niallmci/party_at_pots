North Clare

Ennistymon (Our Host Town!)

Pot Duggans: The lovely venue that you will all be getting very acquainted with

Cheese Press: Artisan cheese shop/café

Glen Walk: Scenic riverside walk

Lahinch

Surfing: Famous surfing destination, use Bens surf clinic for rentals/forecasts

Dining: Frawley's, Hugo's Bar & Restaurant, Irish Chandelier, The Cottage Café

Cliffs of Moher

Cliffs of Moher: UNESCO Global Geopark, iconic limestone cliffs (Official Website)

Cliff Walk: Walking path from Liscannor to Doolin, we will be doing this the morning after the party, all welcome

The Burren

Lough Avalla Farm Walk: Beautiful walk in the Burren, lovely tea room also

Aillwee Caves: Underground cave system with guided tours (Website)

Poulnabrone Dolmen: Neolithic portal tomb (5,800 years old)

Burren Perfumery: Artisan perfume and cosmetics workshop (Website)

Father Ted's House: Filming location from TV series

Aran Islands

Inis Mór (Inishmore)

Dún Aonghasa (Dún Aengus): Prehistoric clifftop fort

Pol na Beist: Dramatic rock formation (Wormhole)

Bike Rental: Traditional way to explore the island

Teach Nan Phaidi

Other Notable Places

Doolin: Gateway to Aran Islands, Gus O'Connor's Pub, traditional music

Liscannor: Fishing village, Vaughan's Pub, Clahane swimming

Fanore Beach: Blue Flag beach, swimming and walks

Miltown Malbay: Traditional music town, Friel's, An Bacús

Spanish Point: Beach with Spanish Armada history, Armada Hotel

West Clare

Loop Head Area

Loop Head Lighthouse: Historic lighthouse dating to 1670, guided tours available (Website)

Bridges of Ross: Natural sea arches, spectacular birdwatching spot (Info)

Kilbaha Gallery: Contemporary Irish art gallery (Website)

Star Wars filming location: Scene from "The Last Jedi"

Carrigaholt

The Long Dock: Award-winning restaurant specializing in fresh local seafood (Website)

Amelia's Cafe: Local café serving fresh food and drinks (Facebook)

Carrigaholt Castle: 15th-century tower house (circa 1480)

Visit the McInerneys in Suan na Mara

Kilkee

Pollock Holes (Diamond Rocks): Natural swimming pools in rocks

Holly's Café: Local café run by Michelin-trained chefs (Facebook)

Naughton's Steak & Seafood: Popular restaurant and bar (Website)

Kilkee cliff walk

Other Notable Places

Doonbeg - Morrissey's: Traditional pub and restaurant

Kilrush: Dolphin watching tours, Scattery Island, Vandeleur Walled Garden, Beag cafe (lovely little hidden gem)

Further Afield

Limerick

Distance: The greatest city not only in Ireland but in the world, and only 1.5 hours from west Clare.

Galway

Distance: Culturally inferior to Limerick but not a total shithole, 1-2 hours from north Clare

Kerry

Distance: Adjacent county with Ring of Kerry and Dingle Peninsula

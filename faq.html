WHAT TO EXPECT

Basically this is our day 2 its just happening almost a year after our actual wedding (give us a chance to financially recover pls). It will be quite casual, but hopefully lots of fun. Where should I stay?

Most people are staying locally (Ennistymon or Lahinch) in B&Bs or otherwise. There is a nice hotel in the town that is walking distance called "The Falls Hotel", we are staying there. There are rooms reserved under our name til a few weeks before if you call them :-) If you end up getting accommodation a bit further away and are worried about transport home late at night, please get in touch and we can help.

Some suggestions for accommodation:
